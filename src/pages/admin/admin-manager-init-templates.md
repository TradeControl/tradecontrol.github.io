Templates provide a fast, reliable way to set up a business with a clear category tree, meaningful totals, and ready-to-use cash codes.

They are optional, but they provide a strong starting point.

## What templates are

Templates provide a pre-configured starting point for a UK business node:

- category tree
- rollups and totals
- cash codes
- related defaults

You can still customize the system afterwards.

## How templates help

A template installs:

- a clear category structure
- meaningful totals such as turnover and gross profit
- cash codes mapped into the correct areas
- VAT settings that match the chosen configuration
- corporation tax settings where relevant

## Template options

## Minimal Micro Company Accounts [YEAR]

**Best for**:

- very small companies
- simple operations
- side businesses
- dormant companies

**What you get**:

- a minimal profit and loss layout
- simple sales and cost structure
- corporation tax support
- VAT support where applicable

## Standard Micro Company Accounts [YEAR]

**Best for**:

- small businesses that want more reporting detail

**What you get**:

- more detailed sales and direct cost categories
- fuller administration structure
- depreciation categories
- corporation tax support
- VAT support where applicable

## Sole Trader Accounts [YEAR]

**Best for**:

- freelancers
- contractors
- self-employed users
- sole-trader businesses

**What you get**:

- a simple income and expense layout
- drawings and capital introduced
- no corporation tax structure
- optional VAT structure
- a structure aligned to sole-trader reporting

## VAT-aware behavior

Each template adapts automatically to the VAT registered setting.

If VAT registered:

- VAT categories and VAT cash codes remain available

If not VAT registered:

- VAT categories are hidden
- VAT control codes are disabled
- default tax behavior becomes zero-rated

## Sole Trader vs Micro Entity

The system supports both business types using the same core data approach.

- sole traders do not use corporation tax
- companies do use corporation tax

## Abridged vs Full Accounts

The same underlying data can support:

- internal management reporting
- micro-entity accounts
- abridged accounts
- fuller reporting outputs

Templates provide the starting structure. Reporting layers are applied on top.
